## SlowMo

**Category:** Media / Video Editing

**SlowMo** is a video editing iOS application that allows users to create and edit videos directly on their device. The app provides a full set of editing tools including trimming, cropping, rotating, adding music, and overlaying text‚Äîenabling users to produce polished videos with ease.
