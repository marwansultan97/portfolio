**FitSync** is an iOS application that enables users to seamlessly synchronize health data from the Fitbit platform into Apple Health. The app supports both day-by-day syncing and large-scale bulk imports‚Äîallowing users to transfer monthly or annual health data using CSV files exported from Fitbit.

With a strong user base and high App Store ratings, FitSync focuses on reliability, performance, and accurate health data mapping, providing users with full control over their health history across platforms.
